VALLEY

A valley or date is a low area between hills, often with a river running through it, a depression that is longer than its wide. Most valleys belong to the main types which are either Glacial or Hanging valleys.

TYPES OF VALLEYS

  • U-shaped valley
  • Through valley
  • Tunnel valley
  • Hanging valley
  • Valley floors

EXAMPLES OF VALLEYS

  • Great Rift Valley
  • Nile valley
  • Jordan valley
  • Sangla valley
  • Valley of Queme

IMPORTANCE OF VALLEYS

  • Agrecultural development
  • Expand settlement
  • Changes in global system
